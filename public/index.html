About Besorah Yeshua

Besorah Yeshua (Gospel of Jesus) is a free Bible reading planner designed to help you stay consistent in your daily Scripture reading. Whether you follow the Ethiopian calendar or the Gregorian calendar, our planner adapts to your needs.

Built with love for the global body of Christ, this tool is completely free and requires no registration. Your reading progress is saved locally in your browser, ensuring your privacy.
